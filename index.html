Get your medical id!

We never know when we might be involved in a serious accident
or succumb to a medical emergency.
If you are overcome by one of these situations,
wouldn’t it be a comfort to know that emergency responders
and health care providers had the information they required in order to save your life?
A emergency medical ID is just like any other
form identification card but it specifically contains information about
your medical conditions or medication allergies. Medical identification cards can also
include emergency contacts, food allergies,
current prescribed drugs, and blood type. During an emergency,
they can provide valuable lifesaving information.
The main purpose of medical ID is to act as a basic source
of medical-related information in case of emergency.
